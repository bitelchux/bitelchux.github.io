📖How to Play

A single deck of cards is your dungeon.

Goal

Clear the deck without dying. Any room card left unplayed is added back once the room refills to just one card.

The Suits

  • ♠️♣️ Monsters — deal damage equal to their value if you fight them.
  • ♦️ Weapons — equip to reduce monster damage; can only fight weaker monsters than the last one it defeated.
  • ♥️ Potions — restore health, but only one works per room.

Rules

  • Each room has 4 cards. Play at least 3 of them (or run) to draw a new room.
  • You can run from a room instead of playing, but not twice in a row.
  • Fight monsters bare-handed (full damage) or with your equipped weapon.
  • Clear the whole deck and the last room to win.

Infinite Mode

Clear one dungeon and descend to the next, tougher one. Random events (chests, traps, mini-bosses, curses), combos, bonus score and achievements only appear here — Classic Mode stays pure.
